ABOUT
I went to school in Boston for Finance because I thought I wanted to become a wealth advisor. Instead, I got a sales job because I wanted to move to New York, and that was the best gig I could get. Now I cast Javascript spells to conjur mythical looking web applications.
As you can tell I have jumped around quite a bit but now I know that I am in the right place! I have always loved technology and been inspired by the idea of creation. Finance and sales were great and all but where was the end result? Where was the tangible THING to show off or use at the end?
Well after a nearly 8 month transition I managed to land my first engineering job and I am learning everyday! I am lucky to work alongside talented, kind, and creative individuals who are in turn making me a better developer and hopefully that starts to show itself here.
